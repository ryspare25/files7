# Slack Deficits Cascade Across Flavors

**Q:** Can a deficit in one slack flavor cause deficits in others?

**A:** Yes. Slack deficits are not independent:

- Low **financial** slack → chronic stress → reduced **emotional** slack.
- Low **space** slack (dense urban living) → inability to own a car → reduced **mobility** slack.
- Low **financial** slack → inability to move → reduced **social** slack (can't relocate to better network).

**Implication:** Interventions on a single slack bottleneck can have outsized cross-flavor effects. Conversely, a location or lifestyle that imposes multiple slack constraints simultaneously (e.g. expensive dense city) compounds dysfunction nonlinearly.

*Source: johnswentworth, LessWrong, 29 Jul 2021*
