# Five Flavors of Slack

**Q:** What are the five canonical types of slack?

**A:**
| Type | Definition |
|------|-----------|
| **Financial** | Money unbudgeted or easily redirected |
| **Time** | Time unscheduled or easily rescheduled |
| **Space** | Space unused or easily repurposed |
| **Emotional** | Capacity to absorb additional stress |
| **Social** | Multiple fallback social groups, or ability to form new ones quickly |

*Source: johnswentworth, LessWrong, 29 Jul 2021*
