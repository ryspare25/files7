# Slack in X-Risk / EA Contexts

**Q:** Why is the slack-externality problem especially acute in x-risk or EA communities?

**A:** Two compounding factors:

1. **Cultural pressure against slack:** The emphasis on "doing the Most Important Thing" and "making large bets" actively discourages maintaining slack — it looks like waste or insufficient commitment.

2. **Irregular high-value opportunities:** X-risk work involves exactly the kind of noisy, unpredictable opportunities (rapid response projects, pivots, collaborations) that require slack to exploit. Slack-poor communities systematically miss these.

**Result:** Communities optimizing hardest for impact may, through anti-slack culture, undermine their own capacity for the highest-impact actions.

*(Elizabeth, 2021 Review)*
