THE TL;DR STRATEGY GUIDE TO NOT BEING A DUMBASS IN BATTLE (AND LIFE)

    Napoleon’s Move – Dude was getting attacked, but instead of just defending like a little bitch or going full Leroy Jenkins on the enemy, he flipped the script. He baited them into thinking he was retreating or losing, then turned around and wrecked their morale by counterattacking when they were at their weakest.

Lesson: If you’re always offensive, people prepare for you. If you’re always defensive, people walk all over you. The key is knowing when to switch.
