
    The Samurai and Zen Perspective – Westerners tend to think in terms of brute force: if someone pushes, you push back. Samurai (and smart fighters in general) were on some galaxy-brain shit, using their opponent’s own strength against them.

Instead of resisting an attack, they’d redirect the energy back at the attacker. Like, why waste your own energy stopping someone’s force when you can just trip them with their own momentum and make them eat dirt?
