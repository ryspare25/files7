Making Sure the Ratification Sticks

If you’re gonna ratify something, a few things gotta line up:

    Your Guy Has to Be Acting for You

If one of your guys claims he’s acting on your behalf, fine—you can approve it.

But if he never even mentioned you in the deal, then too bad—he’s flying solo, and you can’t ratify shit.

Example: If Mikey Meatballs buys a shipment of whiskey saying it’s for himself, then later tries to say it was actually for the boss, the boss can’t ratify that. Mikey owns that mess.
