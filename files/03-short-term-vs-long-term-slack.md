# Short-Term vs Long-Term Slack

**Q:** What is the difference between short-term and long-term slack, and why does it matter?

**A:** Each flavor of slack has a short-term (liquid/quickly deployable) and long-term (illiquid/slowly deployable) dimension.

- A bank with large capital but low liquidity: high long-term financial slack, low short-term financial slack.
- Someone with savings but spending all income: short-term financial slack, no long-term financial slack.

**Why it matters:** Short-term slack determines a group's *agility* — its ability to respond to crises or execute on-the-fly plans. Long-term slack determines sustained capacity.
