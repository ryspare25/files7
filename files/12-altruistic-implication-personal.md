# Personal Altruistic Implication of Slack Externalities

**Q:** If slack creates positive externalities, what is the altruistic individual's prescription?

**A:** Maintain *more* slack across all flavors than pure self-interest would dictate, because the surplus value flows to your groups:

- Keep financial, time, space, emotional, and social slack above your personal optimum.
- Prefer to avoid — or actively supplement — people with very low slack along one or more dimensions (easy for financial, hard for time).
- Recognize that your slack is a gift to the groups you participate in, largely invisible and uncompensated.

**Elizabeth's addition:** In x-risk contexts especially, pressure to "do the Most Important Thing" actively crowds out slack — which then causes high-value irregular opportunities to be missed.

*Source: johnswentworth / Elizabeth, LessWrong, 29 Jul 2021*
