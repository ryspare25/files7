'NAG-CROSS OVER NA TO HAZING'

Inirekomenda ng PNP-CIDG na sampahan ng reklamo si resigned Ateneo men's basketball head coach Tab Baldwin at 10 pang iba sa paglabag sa Anti-Hazing Act kaugnay sa pagkasawi ng student-athletes na sina Rene Baterbonia and

Divine Adili.

"Ang dating iniisip natin, ang hazing ay initiations lamang. Ngunit, dito, ang team-building at training ay nag-crossover papunta sa hazing," saad ni DILG Sec. Jonvic Remulla sa isang press briefing ngayong Biyernes, June 26.

"The supposed team-building activity conducted in Aurora was precisely made to determine which members of the team will make it to the final list submitted by coach Tab Baldwin to the UAAP board," giit naman ni DILG Asec. Brian Mey Tomas.
