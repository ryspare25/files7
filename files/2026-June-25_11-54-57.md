 Why rollback equilibria are so powerful

They automatically eliminate “unreasonable threats.”

They treat every player as perfectly rational at every node, no matter how unlikely that node is to be reached.

They let earlier movers simulate later movers’ brains, choose actions that survive those simulations, and thereby lock in consistent expectations.

In short, the technique provides each player with a crystal-ball view of the future, and the strategies that survive that crystal-ball test form both the rollback equilibrium and the roadmap for the unique path of actual play.
