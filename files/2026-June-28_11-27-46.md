**2. **ESTABLISHING DOMINANCE – SIGNALING WHO’S IN CHARGE

Game theory isn’t just about being nice—it’s also about knowing when to establish dominance. PM did this by calling out HR’s incompetence in a public forum (the group chat), ensuring that everyone knew who was really in charge. By doing so, he didn’t just solve the immediate problem—he set a precedent for future interactions. This is a classic game theory move: make it clear that if anyone tries to undermine you, they’re going to get shut down hard. It’s about sending a signal that your authority isn’t to be questioned.
