# Group Crises Demand Multiple Slack Flavors Simultaneously

**Q:** Why do group crises or large coordinated actions require slack across *multiple* dimensions at once?

**A:** Any significant group action typically requires short-term slack across several flavors simultaneously:

- **COVID rapid-response projects** (microcovid, radvac, forecasting): required financial slack (pause/quit job), emotional slack (high stress), and social slack (coworkers ≠ only friends).
- **Organization relocation** to another city/country: required financial slack (moving costs), emotional slack (moves are among the most stressful life events), and social slack (friends in new location, or ability to form new ones).

**Implication:** A group's effective capacity for decisive action is bounded by the *minimum* slack dimension among its members.

*Source: johnswentworth, LessWrong, 29 Jul 2021*
